Chapter 2: Big Data Lifecycle¶

In the previous chapter, we introduced the concept of Big Data and its importance in today’s data-driven world. We also discussed the challenges associated with managing and analyzing Big Data. In this chapter, we will explore the Big Data lifecycle, which encompasses the various stages involved in collecting, processing, and analyzing Big Data. Also, we will cover key aspects of the Big Data lifecycle: data storage, data security and privacy, and data governance and management. By understanding the Big Data lifecycle, organizations can effectively manage their data and derive valuable insights to drive informed decision-making.

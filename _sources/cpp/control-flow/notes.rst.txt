Loops
=====

Compare while, do-while, and for
--------------------------------

while and for can do everything; do-while run at least one round

Off by one problem in for loops
-------------------------------

Check your first value and last value in your loop. Also known as conner
cases.

Break, continue and return
--------------------------

Modify the flow of control in a loop

:break: jump out of the inner most loop
:continue: jump to the start of the next round in the loop
:return: jump out of the function
